ABOUT THIS PROJECT.

The Criminal Minds behind CRIMETIME

By gathering APIs and Open Data from survelliance cameras, police stations, hospitals, webbcameras and recent crimescenes in Stockholm we have created an app to help you plan your perfect crime! Here you can see where there are not any cameras in town. When new crimes are committed you know the police are heading that way so you can take another route. Use the webbcameras to stalk your victims.

Our intention with this app is for it to be used as a safety app, if you are walking home scared to be robbed, take the route with survelliance cameras, walk close to a police station or hospital. Also, by making this app we hope the police will spot the most dangerous places in Stockholm, helping them helping you from getting into trouble.










CRIMETIME Beta Version

We are three students of the Digital Data Strategist program of Hyper Island in Stockholm. During our "Toolbox" module, we receieved a brief for a project we had to accomplish. The brief was as follows:

You have 3.5 weeks to create a web-based app based on Open Data that will benefit the city of Stockholm.
The required features are:
Interactivity.
Get data from at least 2 different sources.
Must be Open Source.
Be hosted online
