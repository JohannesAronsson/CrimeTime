ABOUT THIS PROJECT.

The Criminal Minds behind CRIMETIME

By gathering APIs and Open Data from surverlliance cameras, police stations, webbcameras and recent crimescenes in Stockholm we have created an app to help you plan your crime! Here you can see where there aren’t any cameras in town, and when it’s a new crime you know the police are heading that way – so you can take another route.
Our intention with this app is for it to be used as a safety app, if you are walking home scared to be robbed, take the route with survelliance cameras, walk close to a police station or hospital. Also we hope that by making this app the police will see where the most dangerous places are in Stockholm, and that way try to prevent crimes and areas that are in the zone.
